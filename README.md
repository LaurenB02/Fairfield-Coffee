# Fairfield Coffee 

## Creator

Lauren Burg

## Description

This project is a brand new website for a local coffee shop. They do not have a current website for their shop and are currently running things on Facebook and Instagram. The new website will showcase their menu items, hours, and location along with new items and monthly specials. This will help new customers and returning ones to be able to easily access important information on the shop.

The site will include aestheic imagery that matches the style of the shop and clear accessibility to information. The goal is to showcase what the store has to offer to bring in more happy customers.

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** People who like coffee, fine goods, specialty drinks, breakfast items, lunch items, and a calm atmosphere.
  
- **Secondary:** Supporters of local and small businesses that would rather shop at small businesses rather than big chain ones.

## Project Goals

- Design a fluid and aesthetic user-friendly site that accurately displays the necessary information for the shop.
- Showcase a pleasing image layout to relay menu items in an appealing way.
- Include hours and location in a simplistic way that makes it easy and quick to find.
- Present an About Us that can reflect the origins of the shop to bring customers closer to local businesses.
